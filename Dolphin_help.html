How to Help

Sponsor a Baby.

It cost an average of $300 per month to keep a baby at Dolphin Foundation, but partial sponsorships of $50, $100 or other amounts are gratefully welcomed too. We are often asked why so much? Babies are expensive! Formula is expensive even in Africa! We are not a “dirt floor” orphanage, we have babies who are premature and need sterile environments or babies who have special needs and all Dolphin Foundation babies have lost their mothers and need extra loving care. Dolphin Foundation babies are well fed, happy, loved and in a clean and nurturing environment. Keeping up with Your Sponsored Baby.

Dolphin Foundation is getting close to 400 babies who have come through our program and average between 50 to 60 babies each month living at Dolphin Foundation.
